Before you start (2 notes)

Keep it safe and avoid confusion

Capture only on a network you own/have permission to test.

Home Wi‑Fi is perfect for learning.

Most sites use HTTPS, so you’ll see TLS.

That’s normal: the “outside labels” are still visible.

Action steps (do this exactly)

1

3

5
