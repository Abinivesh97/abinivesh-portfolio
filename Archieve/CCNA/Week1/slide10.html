Why two models exist

A beginner-friendly way to organize “what happens”

OSI = a teaching blueprint

Splits networking into 7 layers so it’s easier to learn.

TCP/IP = how it's actually built

Real networks group those ideas into 4 practical layers.

Good news

They describe the same journey—just with different “grouping.”

Beginner memory trick

OSI is more detailed (7). TCP/IP is more used (4). They describe the exact same journey.
