Layer 7: Application — The User’s Doorway

The layer you interact with (apps & websites)

OSI Layer 7 of 7

Simple analogy

Like writing a letter and putting it in an envelope

You create the message.

Example: “Open a website,” “send an email,” “join a game.”

This is the “front door” of networking.

Everything feels like buttons and screens at this layer.

Ports help apps find the right “service.”

Think of a port as the department number inside a big building.
