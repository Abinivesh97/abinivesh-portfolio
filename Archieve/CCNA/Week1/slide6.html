Layer 4: Transport — The Shipping Department

Breaks a big message into smaller pieces and helps them arrive (or not) safely

OSI Layer 4 of 7

Simple analogy

Like a shipping department splitting one big box into smaller boxes

Job #1: Split data into chunks called segments.

Smaller pieces are easier to send, track, and re-send if needed.

Job #2: Decide “reliable” vs “fast” delivery.

That choice is usually TCP (reliable) or UDP (fast).

Job #3: Use ports to reach the right app.

Ports are like “department numbers” inside a building.
